About

Here we talk about various variables that are present in this project:

  1. Gender: choose between Male and Female
  2. Race/Ethnicity: This feature takes into account the race or ethnicity of the student. These are:
    1. Group A
    2. Group B
    3. Group C
    4. Group D
    5. Group E
    Obviously we have hidden the actual race/ethnicity so as to not promote any racism.
  3. Parental Level of Education: This feature takes into account the education level of parents. The options to choose from are:
    1. Associate's Degree
    2. Bachelor's Degree
    3. High School
    4. Master's Degree
    5. Some College
    6. Some High School
  4. Lunch Type: There are 2 lunch options to choose from:
    1. Standard Lunch
    2. Free/Reduced
  5. Test Course: Test Course is either Completed or None
  6. Writing Score: Choose the score out of 100
  7. Reading Score: Choose the score out of 100
The link to Kaggle dataset is: Student Performance Dataset
